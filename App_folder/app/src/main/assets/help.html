Some FAQs

  • HOW TO ADD ITEMS TO THE LIST ?

    For adding the item to the bill list the seller needs to click on the respective item that he/she wants to add, present on the available list.

  • HOW TO REMOVE AN ITEM FROM THE BILL LIST ?

    To remove an item from the bill list the seller needs to click on the remove button, present against the quantity of the respective item.

  • WHAT IS THE FUNCTIONALITY OF BILL SPLIT ?

    On the occasion of a group visiting the business, our software provides an exclusive option of splitting the bill among the group members, for this the shopkeeper needs to enter the number of divisions of bills required in the "Split amomg people ? " Option.

  • WHAT TO DO NEXT ?

    Once a desired bill has been set up by the seller the next step is to get the GST invoice, for doing so he/she needs to press the print button, on doing so the GST invoice for the items purchased will be saved in the sellers device and later can be shared with the customer as hard or soft copy.
